Explanation

The goal of Three's Away is to get the lowest score. Every time you roll the die, you have to choose a die to keep. Green dice are clickable, red aren't. Click on a die to keep it and add it to your score. If you roll a three you must keep it. After keeping a die, you can roll the rest of your dice. Repeat until you no longer have any more dice. After player 1 is done, it's player 2's turn. Remember, whoever has the lowest score wins!
